Production and arrangement

We help shape your musical idea into something clearer, stronger and more focused.

Mix

Send us your tracks and we will work toward a balanced, deep and defined mix.

Stereo master

We give your track the final treatment needed for release and playback across different systems.

Stem mastering

When more control is needed, we can work from grouped stems for a more precise result.

An online, human and professional service

Geco Studio works online: you send us your material over the internet, we work on it in the studio, and deliver it back to you in a simple and direct way.

Our approach is not based on impersonal automation or AI-driven mixing. We bring listening, taste, experience, technique and musical judgment.

If needed, we are also available for phone or video calls, so we can better understand your project, expectations and artistic direction.

Experience, digital tools and analog outboard

Geco Studio was founded in 2026, but it is built on nearly two decades of experience in music production.

The studio uses both high-level digital tools and analog outboard gear to deliver a careful, flexible and result-oriented service.
