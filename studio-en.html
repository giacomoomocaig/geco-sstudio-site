The studio

Approach, experience and attention to sound

Geco Studio was created to offer online music production, mixing and mastering with a technical, musical and human approach.

Go to services Contact us

A concrete view of audio work

Geco Studio was founded in 2026, but it is built on nearly two decades of experience in music production.

The goal is not only to achieve a technically correct sound, but to truly enhance the character of the track, its message and its intention.

An online service, but not an impersonal one

The work is carried out online: the material is sent over the internet, worked on in the studio and delivered back in a simple and direct way.

That does not mean a cold or automatic service. On the contrary, Geco Studio values discussion, listening and real understanding of the project.

If needed, we are also available for phone calls or video calls to better define expectations, sonic aesthetics and project goals.

No shortcuts, no soulless processes

Our approach is not based on automated mixes or on processes handled by artificial intelligence.

We bring listening, taste, technical skill, experience and sensitivity. Every project is handled with real attention, not with the same impersonal chain for everyone.

Digital and analog

Geco Studio uses high-level digital tools together with analog outboard gear, choosing what is truly needed for each project.

The focus is always on the final result: a sound that works well, keeps its identity and translates properly across different listening systems.

Who Geco Studio is for

The service is intended for artists, producers, musicians and audio projects that need professional support in production, mixing or mastering.

But even non-musicians with audio-related needs are welcome to get in touch and see whether the service fits their project.

Let’s talk about your project

If you want to understand whether Geco Studio is the right fit for your work, write to us and tell us what you are looking for.
