Production and arrangement

If you have a musical idea to develop, we can help turn it into a clearer, stronger and more coherent track. From creative support to arrangement, we help give your music a clear direction.

Mix

Mixing is the stage where all tracks find balance, depth, space and impact. We work to enhance every element of the song and bring out its sonic character the right way.

With our mix service you receive:

  • 1 final file of your track at 48 kHz / 24 or 32 bit, depending on the project
  • 1 folder containing grouped stems

Delivery time depends on the number of tracks involved.

Stereo master

Mastering is the final step that prepares your music for release and playback across different listening systems. The goal is a solid, coherent result ready for digital platforms.

With our stereo mastering service you receive:

  • 1 file at 48 kHz / 24 bit for platforms that accept high resolution
  • 1 file at 44.1 kHz / 16 bit, the standard playback format
  • 1 MP3 file at 320 kbps for compatible platforms and uses

Delivery is generally within 5 working days.

Stem mastering

When more control is needed than with a simple stereo master, we work with grouped stems to refine balance, dynamics and overall impact more precisely.

Stem mastering files should not exceed 6 stems and must be clearly labeled.

Revisions and delivery times

We generally include 3 revisions. If more changes are needed, they can be discussed afterwards.

Delivery times vary depending on the requested service and project complexity.

How to work together effectively

To achieve the best result, it is important to send well-organized material, properly exported and accompanied by clear indications about the sound you want.

You can include:

  • a rough mix reference
  • a song by an artist whose sound you like
  • a short description of the emotion or message you want to deliver
